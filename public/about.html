About the Game

Welcome to the brainchild of many caffeine-fueled coding sessions: "In Their Own Words." Born out of a desire to make IMA Low Res cohort bonding more amusing, this trivia web app puts a new twist on the usual dragonfruit camaraderie, infusing it with never-before-heard fun facts and a touch of friendly competition.

Crafted with the magic of Node.js, Sockets.io, Express.js and MongoDB, we've turned icebreakers from cringe-worthy to slightly less cringe-worthy. Not exactly quantum physics—well, maybe just a sprinkle. Let the connections unfold!
